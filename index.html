Overview of Speech

This page contains three main sections:

  • Original Speech: contains the raw speech samples sourced from the Fujitsu Japanese Emotional Speech Database.
  • Noise Vocoded Speech (0-64 Hz): contains the noise-vocoded speech of original speech, with the modulation frequency components in the 0-64 Hz band.
  • Noise Vocoded Speech (0-16 Hz): contains the noise-vocoded speech of original speech, with the modulation frequency components in the 0-16 Hz band.
